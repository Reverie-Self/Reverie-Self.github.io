I used to explode—ignite on command
A flash, a roar, just smoke in their hands
Thought I was the bomb, thought I made them shake
But all I left were echoes, just another mistake

Stopped and stared—what did I find?
A wasteland carved from the wreckage inside
Take care, take care—your actions remain
Every ember still whispers my name

So I turned inward, nowhere to go
Detonation—imploding slow
Scars uncoiled like venom in veins
Burning through me, but no one remains

Weak, I sit—hollowed and torn
Strength, I seek—but I'm weathered, worn
Pain’s the only ghost that stays
Ripping through me in endless waves

The fire is fed, the wound is wide
Flesh from soul, no place to hide
Memory slips like smoke in rain
Will I ever feel again?

Detonate, detonate—fire in my bones!
Let it burn or turn to stone!
Ashes settle, but I still breathe
Am I lost, or am I free?

I used to explode… now I just fade…
